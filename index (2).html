Nincasa was born from a simple belief: great food deserves great beer, and great beer deserves great food. Our kitchen draws from global traditions, while our brewery pushes the boundaries of what craft beer can be.

Every dish is prepared with locally sourced ingredients. Every beer is brewed on-site, steps from your table. The result is a dining experience you will not find anywhere else in Pune.

The Brewery

Brewed Here.
Poured Here.

Our 5-hectolitre brewhouse sits behind glass walls in the heart of the restaurant. You can watch your beer being made while you eat. We brew small batches — typically 200 litres at a time — which means our taps rotate weekly and every pint is fresh.

Golden Haze IPA 6.8% ABV Tropical, hazy, unfiltered
Oak & Ember Stout 7.2% ABV Smoked, rich, dark chocolate
Citrus Wit 4.9% ABV Coriander, orange peel, crisp
Monsoon Ale 5.5% ABV Spiced, warming, seasonal
