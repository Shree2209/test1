The Brewery

Brewed on Site.
Poured at Your Table.

Our 5-hectolitre brewhouse is the heart of Nincasa. You can see it, smell it, and taste the difference.

Small Batches,
Big Character

We brew 200 litres at a time. That is small enough to experiment, large enough to serve our guests. Our brewmaster, Rohan Desai, has been brewing for 12 years — first in Bangalore, then Belgium, and now here in Pune.

The brewhouse sits behind glass walls in the centre of the restaurant. You can watch the entire process: mashing the grains, boiling the wort, adding hops, fermenting, and conditioning. There are no secrets here. Just malt, water, hops, yeast, and time.

5 HL Brewhouse Capacity
200 L Batch Size
14 Taps on the Bar
2-4 Weeks Fermentation
